Survival

This line graph shows the survival rate of the mice over time. The mice that have the highest survival rate for the longest time are the mice treated with Ramicane and Capomulin, which show a fairly steady survival rate between 25 and 20% across all 50 days of the study. This makes these two drugs good candidates for further study.
